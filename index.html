Guessing Game

Welcome to the guessing game. We will generate a number for you between 1 and 100. As soon as you hit the "play" button, you will have 10 seconds to make a guess. Guess correctly to win. If you guess incorrectly, we will tell you if your guess was too high or too low and you will lose a life. You only have 7 lives. If you wait too long, you will lose a life. If you lose all your lives, it's game over.

You can exit the game at any time by clicking on the Exit Game button. Exiting the game will close this tab in your browser. You can also restart the game at any time by clicking on the Restart Game button.
